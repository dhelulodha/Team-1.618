    About SRM

    The SRM University (Sri Ramaswamy Memorial University), formerly SRM Institute of Science and Technology, is a deemed university in the state of Tamil Nadu, India. It was founded in 1985 as SRM Engineering College in Kattankulathur, under University of Madras. It has 7 campuses, 4 in Tamil Nadu-Kattankulathur, Ramapuram, Vadapalani, Tiruchchirappalli and others in NCR, Sonepat and Gangtok. The institute gained deemed status during the 2003-2004 academic year and was renamed SRM Institute of Science and Technology. It became SRM University in 2006, when it attained the status of a full-fledged university, under section 3 of the UGC Act 1956. The university is approved by the University Grants Commission and is accredited by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC).It has been given an overall'A' Grade by NAAC.
    SRM not only focuses on academics but also helps, supports and funds many student teams. It has provided the students with labs and workshops. The faculty members are one of the most skilled and technical faculties out there, who provide theoretical, practical knowledge with guidance.

    About Team

    TEAM 1.618 is the Official Hybrid Vehicle Racing Team of SRM University, going to participate in Hybrid Vehicle Challenge-2016/17 organised by Imperial Society of Innovative Engineers (ISIE).
    The Team was formulated on 14th August 2014 & is a multi-disciplinary group of engineering & technology students, from different branches of engineering namely Automobile, Mechanical, Mechatronics, Electrical, Computer Science and Electronics. The team is guided and mentored by faculty advisors of the university. The team will design and fabricate a Hybrid Vehicle under the ISIE restrictions so as to compete with other teams all over the country. Teams will use an Internal Combustion Engine as well as an Electric Motor to run their Vehicles.

    The objective of the competition is to design and fabricate a hybrid Vehicle or an electrical vehicle under ISIE Design restriction so as to give the chance to students to demonstrate and prove their creativity and their engineering skills in comparison to teams from other universities or colleges around the Asia. Teams will use Internal Combustion Engine as well as Electric motor to run their Vehicle which is called Hybrid vehicle or a vehicle
    that is charged from an electrical source and propelled by electric drive only which is called an electrical vehicle. This will help them face real-world engineering design projects and other related challenges. Our focus to develop interest among the engineering student towards alternative power sources, those which are the future of Automobiles.
