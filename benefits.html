Benefits of Sponsorship

Team 1.618 is the official Formula Hybrid Racing Team of SRM University, participating in the event called Hybrid Vehicle Challenge 2016/17 hosted by Imperial Society of Innovative Engineers (ISIE). Sponsoring our team, your company will have the following benefits:



  Publicity at Ampa Skywalk:

The unveiling of the car will be done in a Mall such as - EA Mall, Chennai. Earlier this year it is done in the Ampa Skywalk. As the Unveiling of our first car is very important to us, we will put all our efforts to make it a successful event. During the unveiling, every team member will be wearing team t-shirts having sponsor logos at the back and we will have banners displaying the logo of each sponsor that we have. At the Unveiling, we will be asking common people some general questions about the Hybrid Technology and its advancements to spread the message of Eco - Green in the society. We will also be giving out an invitation to all our Sponsors for the unveiling of our project car.

  Special Thanks in Videos:

For the promotion of our team and our project car, we will be making various videos such as - Trailer (before the unveiling), Fabrication Video (before the event) and Unveiling (after video). In all those videos, we will be thanking all our sponsors and Company's logo will be displayed in all those videos.

  Individual Banners:

During the event, we will be displaying the banners of sponsor near the paddock area and extensive promotion will be done to publicise our sponsors by our Corporate Team. As the Hybrid Vehicle Challenge is the biggest Hybrid Racing Event in the whole Asia, lots of Company Delegates will be coming to the event to keep an eye on the good teams. Many companies will thus come to know about the partnership of your company with our team. This will thus set off a message that Company's goodwill and gesture towards the student projects as well as Hybrid Technology.

  International Competitions:

After this year's event, we are planning to participate in an International Event. The most renowned event is the Formula Hybrid, USA. Thus all our sponsors will be getting foreign exposure in terms of publicity. The company's being a well-set company in India will be famous in the western hemisphere also.

  Vehicle Testing:

During the testing phase of our project car in MMRT Chennai, we will be inviting one of the officials from your company to be the witness of the event. An exclusive interview with your official will be taken about his thoughts on the testing and hopes from the car. Through this, Company's will become the sole company supporting us in the testing phase and will be a positive note on showing the good relations between us & our new sponsors.

  College Exposure :

SRM University has a big name and so does it fests. We have two major fests namely - AARUUSH (Techno-management Fest) and Milan(Cultural Fest). All the teams from the University display their vehicles in these fests and so will our team. We will ensure that full branding of our Team and our sponsors are done by the means of these fests. The fest provides extensive outreach to students within the university as well as other universities along with different high profile officials from different industries. The fest is the best opportunity for promotion within the university.

  Automotive Testing Expo:

After the event, our vehicle will be displayed in the Automotive Testing Expo India to be held in Chennai. Automotive Testing Expo provides a highly flexible environment in which a wide range of sales and marketing objectives can be achieved and the same can be used for building brand image.

  Green Mobility Expo:

Also the teams with the most aesthetic vehicle and sort the out presentation about their team will get a chance to display their vehicle in International Green Mobility Expo next year. It is a perfect platform for our team to fulfil the purpose of promoting our sponsors and will also be helpful to you in spreading the message of Eco Green Technology under the CSR Category. As we have already secured All India 2nd in the virtual round after extensive documentation and theoretical concepts. In Dynamic round we have grabbed a bunch of trophies with us - Best in B Plan and Cost Report, Best in Autocross, First Runner-up in skid Pad Test, Second Runner Up Acceleration Test and Endurance, concluding we stood Second runner-up in the whole event. This reflects that we have high chances to be placed among the top 3 in the next competition thereby getting the above-mentioned opportunity.
